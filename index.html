Cat Catalogue

There are so many cats in the world, why not showcase a couple?

Cats listed here:

Pinky

Pinky is a sweetheart with a golden soul and 19 toes. While his fur is mostly orange, his toes and lips are the pinkest pink around. Honest-to-god they look like pink tictacs. Pinky is not looking for a loving family as he has been "already adopted by the streets".

Squeaky

Named for his signature squeak, don't let this fella fool you - he is the cat's meow. Squeaky was born 30 years ago as the runt of his litter. Though he may be smallest of the bunch (and only surviving member), he has grown a large following among cat enthusiasts. Squeaky has been attributed to the 1998 Fremont Bridge riots that resulted in 12 cats needing to be resuced from the bridge's girders, though his attorneys deny any involvement.

Mema

Mema is a rambunctious female feline raring for a treat or a frolic outdoors. She likes to hunt rabbits and birds but will only eat field mice. A real cat's cat, she is on good terms with all of the other neighborhood kitties.
